Four Seasons Bakery provides a variety of home-baked goods to order. Situated on Nelson Acres, Four Seasons Bakery has access to the freshest ingredients whose taste is rivalled only by the imagination of the designs on beautiful cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and even breads. Although a small variety of goodies are always available, Four Seasons prefers to create unique and specialty items on order. Always willing to partner fresh new flavor combinations with exciting new techniques and design, Four Seasons Bakery can provide all your special occasion and holiday needs.
