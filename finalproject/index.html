Nelson Acres is focused on a sustainable lifestyle that contributes to ecological health that incorporates wildlife preservation into its plan. The location will be near Roanoke, VA. Nelson Acres will predominately be a hobby farm with a focus on being sustainable within five years of creation. The main commercial product will be heritage, foraging swine to high-end restaurants and honey to grocery stores, and You Pick it berries and fruits. After the first five years, will explore adding CSA to the plan. Additionally, the site will include Four Seasons Bakery™, owned and operated by Autumn Williams.

Management Team: Nelson Acres will be run by Heather and Michael. Heather is a USAF veteran who has studied agriculture and animal behaviorism as a hobby for decades. She uses her .25 acre in Fairfax, VA to experiment with different plants over the last ten years. She will be able to access veteran-specific mentorship and grants, specifically from the Veterans to Farmers Program. She also is an excellent writer and will work on the marketing aspect of the farm. Michael is a Network Security Director at a local university, but has a natural talent to repair anything mechanical or design mechanical processes such as irrigation and automatic feeders.
